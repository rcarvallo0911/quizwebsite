WHAT IS A CONSPIRACY THEORY?




Over the decades, conspiracy theories have become more popular in our society. These conspiracy theories are mixed with facts and over imagination. Conspiracy theories are theories that suggest that one secretive group controls and causes events and/or other situations. However, not everyone believes in these conspiracy theories. Usually believers are the uneducated and unstable financially. Although there are many different conspiracy theories in all topics that can affect everyone differently. Believers can be anyone, it is really based on the mentality of the person and the situation. There are some people who are more prone to believing in conspiracy theories. These people lack education like a graduate degree, probably are young or not very involved in society therefore lack experience, and/or are overall very anxious or paranoid person.
