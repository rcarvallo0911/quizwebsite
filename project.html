Mythos

Folklore, Urban Legends, and Conspiracy Theories

About

For a culture to develop and become much more rich and complex,

it is only natural that traditions and stories would have been
passed down from generation to generation. Our project aims at
digging deep into the foundations of these traditions/stories we
have heard since childhood, and enrich our understanding of their
origin. We also have chosen to address much more modern superstitions
and speculations which have taken form in conspiracy theories and urban legends.
Overall, our goal is to detail how various stories of the past & present
have sparked speculation, inspired new traditions, and helped shape our society.

Folklore

Folkore has been manifested through years and years of careful crafting and tradition.

This topic aims at exploring a few stories that were prominent in some cultures, and how these stories helped contribute to enrich their societies and create a more complex society.

Urban Legends

This subtopic discusses how much more modern moments in history which have sparked rumors and inspired fear mongering

This topic could be considered the “modern-day folklore”.

Conspiracy Theories

Typically, when a tragic event happens out of nowhere people will try to find a way to rationalize and make sense of it.

This topic will address how people attempt to explain unexpected events and how we perceive things. This ties in because it is another form of modern speculation, similar to Urban Legends.
