Folklore

Folkore has been manifested through years and years of careful crafting and tradition.

This topic aims at exploring a few stories that were prominent in some cultures,
and how these stories helped contribute to enrich their societies and create a more complex society.


Witches

Witches are amongst the most prominent creatures in folklore as they can be
seen in almost every culture portrayed differently. One thing that is universa
throughout, however, is a negative view on these creatures implying that they
have some sort of malevolent intent. These beliefs have lead to the spawn of many
terrible events in history such as the Salem Witch Trials and has even played a
part in the Spanish Inquisition!

Slavic Folklore

Folklore from other cultures tends to be overlooked as it exists under the shadow of Roman
and Greek mythologies.

However, although these stories are not necessarily prevalent in modern
media, their existence should still be taken into account and they should be treated with the
same respect that stories from Greece and Rome get. Slavic Folklore finds its roots in the
slavic region, and contains various mythological creatures like the Vodyani, Domovoi, and Leshiy.
