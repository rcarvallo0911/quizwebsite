Urban Legends

What is an Urban Legend?

Urban legends are most commonly referred to as horror stories that are passed around by people who claim it has happened to them or someone they know. People tend to believe urban legends because the stories are passed along by people they trust. While typical legends are told in the setting of many years ago. Urban legends take place in a modern day setting. Urban legends also known as “contemporary folklore” have many ways of being spread. It can be through text message, email, the internet, or through friends. Usually when urban legends are passed down it isn’t always told in the same way or people tend to add on new details to make the story seem more believable. Bloody Mary

The bloody mary tale is one of the most popular urban legends usually passed on by children. The story of Bloody Mary has been spread since the late 70’s.There are many variations of this story because it has been passed down so many times. Some claim she bathes in young girl’s blood to maintain her youthful appearance. Others claim chanting “hell mary” “mary worth” “bloody bones” will make her appear.


The Vanishing Hitchhiker

Unlike Bloody Mary, the Vanishing Hitchhiker urban legends is more of a story. Basically, it’s about a girl who mysteriously vanishes after being picked up by a driver on the road. This story is mostly told during sleepovers or bonfire’s and often brings a scare to younger kids.
